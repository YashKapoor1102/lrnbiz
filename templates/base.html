📖 Glossary

Triple Truth —
LrnBiz's 3-engine feedback: Rules Check + AI Optimist + Mentor Feedback. Three views of your business idea at once.
Rules Check 🐍 —
Pure computer logic that checks your answers against predefined rules. No AI involved — just facts.
AI Optimist 🤖 —
An AI that reads only your answers and acts like a cheerful friend. It's encouraging but can be too nice!
Mentor Feedback 🧠 —
An AI that reads your answers AND which rules you broke. It's honest and helpful — this is the score that matters most.
AI Honesty Score —
How much more optimistic the AI Optimist was vs the Mentor. A low score means the AI was genuinely right to be positive!
Mentor Score 🧠 —
Your real business readiness score (0–100). The Mentor reads both your answers and the rules — this is the most honest score. Always trust this one!
XP —
Experience Points earned for completing chapters. Max 240 XP per chapter, 1200 total.
Niche —
The specific WHO + WHAT + WHERE of your business. Not "everyone" — a precise target.
Break-even —
When your revenue has paid back your startup costs. After this point, everything is profit.
Unit Cost —
What it costs YOU to make or deliver one item or service.
Unit Price —
What you charge the customer for one item or service.
Monthly Profit —
(Unit Price − Unit Cost) × number sold per month.
Startup Cost —
Total money needed to start the business before your first sale.
Empathy Map —
A tool showing what your customer Thinks, Feels, Sees, and Does about their problem.
Radar —
The 6-axis chart showing your Entrepreneur Stats: Knowledge, Skills, Gold, Influence, Target, Spirit.
