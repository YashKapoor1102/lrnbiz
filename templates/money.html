💡 Why does this chapter matter?

Without knowing your costs, you might be selling at a loss without realising it! This chapter helps you check if your business can actually make money — before you spend a single penny. 💰

📚 What you'll learn in this chapter:
  • How to price your product using the 3-step formula
  • The difference between cost, price, and profit
  • How to calculate your break-even point
  • What startup costs are and how to plan for them
⚠️ When businesses skip this chapter:
Quibi raised $1.75 billion and launched a video app in 2020 — then shut down in 6 months. Their unit economics were broken from the start: high content costs, a low subscription price, and no clear profit path. This chapter exists so you check the numbers before spending a cent.
